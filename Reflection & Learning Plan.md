### Reflection
In this project the most important skill we learned was computing on the edge, which is quite broad but also quite relevant. We learned applications for a new and emerging industry standard using the NVIDIA Jetson ORIN developer kit. Edge computing is becoming more and more integrated into technology, cutting costs, reducing data travel and processing time, and bringing a lot of computational power directly to the core of the situation. We expanded on our C programming by learning CUDA code, architecture, and parallel patterns. The skills learned in this project will be incredibly useful to us as students in the embedded systems stream, as they can be the foundation of future projects to come in our academic careers.
